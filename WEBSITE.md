## Brand Guidelines

### Visual Identity — Two Distinct Themes

The brand assets show two parallel visual directions. The website may use either or blend both.

#### Theme A — Dark Gold (Images 1 & 2)
Premium, modern, dark-luxury aesthetic.

| Role | Hex | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Background | `#0e0e0e` | Page background |
| Surface / Card | `#181818` | Section backgrounds, cards |
| Gold (Primary) | `#c9a025` | Headings, icons, CTAs, borders |
| Gold (Hover) | `#e0b84a` | Interactive hover states |
| Body Text | `#f0ece3` | Primary readable text |
| Muted Text | `#a89f8f` | Subheadings, secondary copy |
| Text on Gold | `#0e0e0e` | Text inside filled gold buttons |

#### Theme B — Earthy Parchment (Image 3)
Warm, natural, grounded aesthetic — evokes instinct, nature, the wild.

| Role | Approximate value | Usage |
|---|---|---|
| Background | `#d4c4a8` (parchment) | Page / section background |
| Surface | `#c2ad8a` | Slightly darker parchment for cards |
| Dark Text | `#2c1f10` (dark brown) | Headings and body |
| Accent | `#4a3520` (deep brown) | Dividers, borders |
| Wolf imagery | Full-colour realistic photo | Hero background or section image |

> **Recommendation:** Use Theme A (dark gold) as the primary website theme for a premium digital feel, and reserve Theme B colour tones for print materials or a secondary "about" section with the wolf photography.

### Typography

| Element | Style |
|---|---|
| Headings | Serif (Georgia / Playfair Display / Times New Roman) — bold |
| Body / UI | Sans-serif (Arial / system-ui) — regular / light |
| Labels / Badges | Sans-serif, uppercase, wide letter-spacing (0.15–0.22em) |
| Italic accent | Used for the "Instinctive…" tagline — serif italic |
| Heading sizes | Fluid: `clamp(1.9rem, 4.5vw, 3.2rem)` H1 · `clamp(1.5rem, 3.5vw, 2.3rem)` H2 |

### Logo Variants

#### Variant A — Gold silhouette on dark (Images 1 & 2)
- Stylised wolf-head facing right (profile / 3/4 view)
- Single-colour gold fill, no outline
- Used without a border ring in Image 1; may appear in a circular badge

#### Variant B — With padlock symbol (Image 2)
- Wolf head + small padlock icon centred below the chin
- Represents the "mind unlock" brand concept
- Gold on dark background

#### Variant C — Dark silhouette on parchment (Image 3)
- Same wolf-head shape, filled dark brown (`#2c1f10`)
- Sits above the wordmark on light/parchment backgrounds

#### Wordmark
- **WOLFMIND** on line 1, **HYPNOTHERAPY** on line 2
- All caps, bold serif, tightly spaced
- Gold on dark (Theme A) / dark brown on parchment (Theme B)

### Aesthetic & Tone
- Calm, trustworthy, professional — not clinical or cold
- Instinctive and natural — the wolf symbolises intuition, focus, and depth
- Premium without being inaccessible
- Copy is first-person, direct, reassuring
- No stage-hypnosis clichés — language is always grounded and therapeutic

## Design Principles

1. **Dark by default** — primary theme uses `#0e0e0e` / `#181818` backgrounds.
2. **Gold as the single accent** — never fill large areas in gold; use it for borders, icons, interactive elements, and labels only.
3. **No forms** — all contact via phone link (`tel:`), email link (`mailto:`), and website link.
4. **Wolf imagery** — the realistic wolf photo (Image 3) may be used as a hero background at low opacity or as a full-bleed section; never use generic stock people photography.
5. **Logo on dark only** — the gold silhouette logo is legible only on `#0e0e0e` or equivalent dark backgrounds; use the dark-brown variant on parchment.
6. **Border-left gold rule** — 3px solid gold left border on cards and callouts instead of drop shadows.
7. **Radial glow sparingly** — max one subtle gold radial gradient per section at 5–8% opacity.
8. **Responsive via flex/grid** — no fixed pixel widths; use `clamp()` for type and `minmax()` for grids.
9. **Accreditation visible** — always show the GHSC accreditation line near the therapist name or in the footer.
