Common questions

What to expect

Will I lose control during hypnosis?

No. You remain fully aware and in control throughout every session. Clinical hypnotherapy is nothing like stage hypnosis or what you may have seen in films. You won't be made to do anything against your will — it's focused, professional therapeutic work, and you can end the session at any time.

How many sessions will I need?

This varies depending on what you're working through. Some clients notice significant shifts after just one or two sessions. Others benefit from a short course of four to six sessions for deeper or more complex issues. We'll discuss this during your free initial consultation.

Can hypnotherapy be done online?

Yes — online sessions are just as effective as in-person ones. All you need is a quiet space, a reliable internet connection, and somewhere comfortable to sit or lie. Many clients find the comfort of their own home actually helps them relax more deeply.

What does hypnotherapy feel like?

Most clients describe it as a state of deep relaxation — similar to that drowsy feeling just before sleep, but with a clear and focused mind. You'll hear everything I say and remain in full control. Afterwards, many people feel calm, lighter, and surprisingly refreshed.

Is hypnotherapy right for me?

If you're open to the process and ready to make a change, hypnotherapy can be remarkably effective. I offer a free initial consultation so we can talk through what you're experiencing and decide together whether it's the right fit — with no pressure or obligation.
